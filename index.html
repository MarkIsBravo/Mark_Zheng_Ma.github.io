About Me

I completed my bachelor's degree in Beijing Langauge and Culture University, majoring in Chinese Language and Literature. It was an unforgettable experience and I developed my interest of being a Mandarin teacher during that time.

Coming to the US was one of the best decisions in my life. I completed my Masters degree of Teaching and Curriculum at Syracuse University and it was an amazing two years. During that time, I explored a lot of resources on how technologies were used in education and experienced how much revolutionary they were to traditional ways of learning.

After graduating from Syracuse University, I came to NYC and became a Mandarin teacher at a private school in Manhattan. It was a precious learning experience but not fulfilling enough for me. The more I experienced, the more I realize that learning technology is the only way to make a leap not only in my teaching work but also in my own life.

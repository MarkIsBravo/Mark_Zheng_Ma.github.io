About Me

I completed my bachelor's degree in Beijing Langauge and Culture University, majoring in Chinese Language and Literature. It was an unforgettable experience and I developed my interest of being a Mandarin teacher during that time.

Coming to the US was one of the best decisions in my life. I completed my Masters degree of Teaching and Curriculum at Syracuse University and it was an amazing two years. During that time, I explored a lot of resources on how technologies were used in education and experienced how much revolutionary they were to traditional ways of learning.

After graduating from Syracuse University, I came to NYC and became a Mandarin teacher at a private school in Manhattan. It was a precious learning experience but not fulfilling enough for me. The more I experienced, the more I realize that I want to design my own educational technology and implement it in the classrooms.

I have passions in being an educator since guiding others to come through difficulties is a satisfying process for me. I like the beauty of Chinese culture and, since Mandarin is becoming more and more popular, I would love to introduce more about this culture to people who have the same passion in learning it.

The WDI program have started for a week and I still feel good about the whole learning process. I learned introductory level skills of HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript and I now am able to create this elegant webpage with the skills so far. I believe I would obtain more and more skills in a few weeks and we will see what new features I could add to this webpage.
